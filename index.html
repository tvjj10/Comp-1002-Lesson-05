Grilled Cheese Recipe

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Bread
  • Tender, Love, and Care
  • Butter
  • Bacon (optional)

Steps

  1. Butter bread
  2. Slice cheese
  3. Face buttered side out and put in sliced cheese
  4. Grill both sides on a griddle or in a frying pan till golden brown
